Demo Call Transcript – Ben's Electric Solutions
Date: January 8, 2026
Participants: Bharat (Clara Solutions), Nick (Clara), Ben Penoyer (Owner, Ben's Electric Solutions)

---

[Context: Introductory demo call. Ben has been running his electrical business for 8 years, electrician for 30 years. Solo proprietor based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.]

Bharat: Ben, how long have you been in this business?

Ben: I've been working for myself for about eight years now. I've been an electrician for 30 years. I just have a couple guys, couple vans out on the road. Do a lot of the admin and back end stuff myself. I've got another guy coming on as operations manager to help me out. His name is Greg.

Bharat: What kind of jobs do you do?

Ben: Everything. Service calls, small jobs, odd jobs. Renovations, troubleshooting. Full projects like new custom homes or tenant improvements. EV chargers, hot tub hookups, panel changes. All that service maintenance stuff. We do residential and commercial.

Bharat: What systems do you use?

Ben: I use Jobber as my CRM. It's fantastic. I also use QuickBooks for accounting and invoicing flows through Jobber.

Bharat: What are your business hours?

Ben: Most calls are business hours. I occasionally get calls after hours and weekends. We're not equipped for emergency services except for some regular property managers and general contractors — we'll get out there for emergency calls for them. If someone I don't know has an emergency call on the weekend, I'll defer them elsewhere.

Bharat: Do you have someone on call after hours?

Ben: I'm the one on call. I have one property manager — they run some gas stations. If their pumps go down, they've got to be fixed now. I get out there for that. I don't have any of my guys on call.

[Live demo of Clara agent performed — Ben called a demo number and tested the AI agent]

Ben: That was actually pretty good. You can tell right away you're talking to a computer but who cares — it answered all my questions. I tried interrupting her. I requested a time that we're not actually open and it handled it. I like that.

Bharat: The system can take 20-30 calls simultaneously, speak multiple languages, and handle smart escalations — routing calls to specific team members based on the type of call.

Ben: How does it handle calls that get routed to me personally?

Bharat: We can configure certain numbers to bypass screening entirely — personal numbers, VIP customers. For others, Clara can screen and ask a qualifying question before routing.

Ben: What's the pricing?

Bharat: Starter plan is $249/month for 500 minutes. We can start with a 3-month plan.

Ben: I'd like to try it for three months. That sounds pretty good.

[Ben agreed to proceed. Kickoff call scheduled for next day, noon.]
